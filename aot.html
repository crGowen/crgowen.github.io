Info

Upon loading, a randomised galaxy of 2500 stars is created, roughly a tenth of which are generated as late stage stars (and have a good chance of going supernova), and somewhere amongst the 2500 stars are 14 civilisations. All civilisations will begin their own version of SETI and will occassionally beam a very narrow but long range beam across the galaxy, if the beam hits another civilisation they may begin a process of establishing contact, the closer a civilisation is, the more likely they are to detect the beam. It typically takes 1000s years for anybody to establish contact with another civilisation, but since it's all randomly generated it's all about probability and could go very differently.

Controls

Mute supernova booms : M
Reset view : R
Generate new galaxy : F5
Change speed : , and .
View civilisation : LMB click on one of the civilisation list
View star : LMB click on star
Rotate view : LMB click and drag
Zoom in or out : W and S (combine with Left Shift to speed up); alternatively use mouse scroll wheel
Move view sidewards : A and D (combine with Left Shift to speed up)
Roll view : Q and E (combine with Left Shift to speed up)
Move "vertically" : Z and X (combine with Left Shift to speed up)
